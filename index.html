  • 療程前2小時避免大量進食。

  • 預留充足時間，放鬆心情來。

常見問題

你可能想知道的

第一次做推拿會痛嗎？Will tuina hurt the first time?

手法力度會因應個人耐受度調整，初次建議輕柔一點，讓身體慢慢適應。Pressure is adjusted to your comfort level. A gentler technique is recommended for first-timers.

精油會引起過敏嗎？Can essential oils cause allergies?

療程前會了解過敏情況，並選用最適合你體質的精油配方，安全第一。Allergy history is checked before each session, and the most suitable oils are selected for your skin type.

懷孕或生理期可以做嗎？Can I come during pregnancy or period?

懷孕初期及生理期建議避免，或事先告知以便調整手法與精油種類。Early pregnancy and menstruation are generally advised to avoid, or please inform in advance to adjust the approach.

多久做一次比較好？How often should I come?

視乎個人狀況，一般建議每月一至兩次作為日常保健，有需要時可增加頻率。Generally once or twice a month for maintenance, with frequency adjusted based on your condition.

需要自備東西嗎？Do I need to bring anything?

唔需要，所有用品都會準備好，你只需要帶著自己來就可以了。No, everything is prepared. Just bring yourself and come with a relaxed mind.

可以指定精油嗎？Can I choose my oils?

當然可以！告知喜好的香氣，我會盡量配合調配最適合你的配方。Of course! Share your scent preferences and I'll blend the most suitable formula for you.
